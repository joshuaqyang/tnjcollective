Executive Team

Josh Mysore

I’m Josh Mysore, from Dallas, TX. As a proud JCamp ‘19 alumnus and the editor of my yearbook, I’ve set my current goal to encompass creating media for people’s needs. TNJC was established with that goal in mind: to be a source of informative news.

Joshua Yang

I’m Joshua Yang, a high school senior based in Palo Alto, California, and I run our editorial team. In the past, my work has been recognized by NCTE and the Scholastic Art & Writing Awards; my writing has also appeared in The Best Teen Writing of 2020.

Olivia Gyapong

I am a senior from Silver Spring, MD. I am editor-in-chief of my high school’s newspaper as well as of a blog I co-founded with fellow YYGS 2019 alumni. I am excited to have yet another outlet in TNJC through which I can inform others and share people’s stories!
